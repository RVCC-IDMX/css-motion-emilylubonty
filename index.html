Accessibility & Reflection

Reduced Motion Implementation

I handled prefers-reduced-motion by using global media queries to reduce or eliminate non-essential animations across all sections. By using global variables, this ensures all sections are accessible to users with motion sensitivities.

Design Reflection

The section that surprised me the most was timing. I experimented with different timing functions and discovered how slow or fast transitions can impact the user experience. I personally feel that the feature card best expresses my intent because it combines both engagement and interactivity. I feel that the flip motion can be useful for revealing additional information without overwhelming the user initially with walls of text or information. I learned that motion is a a design tool that can impact user engagement and overall feel of a website. I also learned that different timing functions can envoke different feelings and responses from users, such as something being too fast or too slow. It is also important to consder the accessibility of motion in design to ensure all users have a positive experience.
